## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will this replace our SDRs?**
A: No. It replaces the boring parts of their job (data entry, research, initial outreach) so they can focus on what humans do best: building relationships and having real conversations.

**Q: What if the AI sends something embarrassing?**
A: It can't. Every campaign goes through the Gatekeeper dashboard where a human must approve before anything sends. You see exactly what will go out.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: Yes. The system has built-in compliance for GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and LinkedIn ToS. It won't let you send without required elements (unsubscribe links, physical address, etc.).

**Q: How long until we see results?**
A: Week 1 is setup. Week 2-3 you'll have campaigns running. Week 4+ you should see meetings being booked. Full optimization takes 2-3 months.

**Q: What happens when someone replies?**
A: The AI classifies the reply. Simple responses (not interested, bad timing, need more info) are handled automatically. Complex responses (pricing, technical questions, interested executives) are routed to the right human immediately.

**Q: Can competitors use this against us?**
A: The system is specific to your ICP, messaging, and processes. It's not a product we're sellingâ€”it's your competitive advantage.
