## Quality Standards

### Required Elements (must have):
- [ ] Meeting time and Zoom link
- [ ] Contact name, title, company
- [ ] At least 1 pain point identified
- [ ] At least 3 discovery questions
- [ ] Clear next step recommendation

### Preferred Elements (should have):
- [ ] Recent LinkedIn activity
- [ ] Company news (last 90 days)
- [ ] Competitive intel
- [ ] Previous conversation summary
- [ ] Mutual connections

### Excellence Elements (nice to have):
- [ ] Personal interests/hobbies
- [ ] Upcoming company events
- [ ] Decision timeline
- [ ] Budget indicators
- [ ] Technical requirements

---

## Failure Handling

If research fails:

1. **Partial data**: Deliver what we have + flag gaps
2. **No data**: Alert AE, suggest postponing if critical
3. **API errors**: Use cached data, note staleness
4. **Time crunch**: Prioritize Quick Glance + Questions

---

## Feedback Loop

After each meeting, capture:

1. **Did the brief help?** (1-5 rating)
2. **What was missing?**
3. **What was inaccurate?**
4. **What was most useful?**

Feed into self-annealing to improve future briefs.
